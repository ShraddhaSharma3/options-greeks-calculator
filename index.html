What Are the Greeks?

Delta If the stock moves ₹1, the option price moves by Delta. Call Delta is always positive (0 to 1), Put Delta negative (-1 to 0).
Gamma How fast Delta itself changes. High Gamma means your Delta changes rapidly — useful near expiry or at-the-money options.
Theta Every day that passes, the option loses Theta in value. Options lose value as time runs out — this is "time decay."
Vega For every 1% rise in implied volatility, the option price increases by Vega. Higher volatility = more expensive options.
